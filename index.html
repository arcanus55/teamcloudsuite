Common Questions

What does Team Cloud Suite Provide?

Team Cloud Suite is an offical Cloud Partner of Google Workspace.

As a Google Workspace Partner, we offer the tools you need to migrate to us with the addition of dedicated account support and a free suite of business productivity tools!

Google relies on companies like us to ensure their users have a smooth Workspace experience.

Whether you are new with Google Workspace, already a user, or with another manager provider, our tool makes it easy to migrate to Team Cloud Suite.

There are no additional costs and you can opt out at any time with the click of the button.

Team Cloud Suite users get Email Reply Time. This tool that allows for instant insights into an organization's reply time habits. Customize your reporting, filter effectively for the cleanest data possible, and quickly spot the weak points in your team's response habits.

Increase your bottom line, streamline interaction touchpoints, and impress your customer base with Email Reply Time.

Our users also get Team Signature Change, a powerful tool that allows for quick and painless edits of email signatures. Easily add new company logos, locations, job titles, or contact information by the individual, a set group, or company-wide.

How does your migration tool work?

For users that already use Google Workspace and want to use our tools for free, they can migrate their Workspace account to us.

Our sign up process will take you step-by-step to locate your unique Workspace token under your Google Admin Account.

You will provide that token to our tool and our application will do the rest.

Your Workspace account will then be associated with Team Cloud Suite and you will get our tools for absolutely zero cost.

What happens when we make Team Cloud Suite our managed provider?

Like all Workspace providers, the Team Cloud Suite app will help you manage forgotten passwords, domain settings, and billing.

Nothing else changes in regards to your Workspace account.

There are no increases in costs for letting Team Cloud Suite be your Workspace manager and users can revert back to normal Workspace accounts with the click of a button.

Google Cloud partners like Team Cloud Suite allow for you to get the care and attention you need from an engaged partner, rather than solely relying on Google for support

We are passionate about creating business productivity tools that utilize the power of Google applications and we offer this suite of tools totally free of charge for our users.

What is the Team Cloud Suite app?

The Team Cloud Suite app is an application that runs on the Google Cloud Platform.

Through the Team Cloud Suite app, we are able to help you manage things like any forgotten passwords, adding or removing users, and more.

Our Google Cloud Platform approved app is works with Google to easily make these changes.

Our app uses the Gmail API to read and analyze email etadata information that we use in our Email Reply Time dashboards.

The data we read is only related to response time metrics and through our Terms of Service agreement with Google, our app cannot read things like email body information or email attachements.

What does it mean to be a Google Cloud Partner?

Google Cloud Partners help Google by offering their expertise in the Google Cloud, Google Workspace, and other Google services.

This allows for the best service for Google customers.

Google Cloud Partners work directly with Google and are thoroughly vetted.

Team Cloud Suite is an approved seller of Google Workspace and we can be found in their seller directory here.

We are also an approved Servicer of Google Workspace and our listing for servicing can be found here.

We are an approved Google Cloud Application Builder and we can be found in the Google Cloud Partner directory here.

How are you able to give users your tool for free?

Google rewards us for being attentive providers of their service so we don't have to worry about marking up your Workspace costs.

As a result of this we are able to offer our tools for free just for allowing us to be your Workspace provider. Everyone wins!


We manage your Workspace license on behalf of Google.

Google then gets our support by allowing us to manage your experience on Google Workspace.

You get an attentive Workspace partner AND access to our business productivity tools. Both for zero extra cost!

Can I cancel at any time?

You can cancel at any time with the click of a button.

Your Workspace license will automatically revert back to Google.

Your billing will then continue to be through Google and you will have no interruption in service.

It's that easy.
