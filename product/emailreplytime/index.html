    Common Questions

    Who is Email Reply Time for?

    Email Reply Time is for those who want instant insights into their email response time analytics.

    Our customers see the value in the revenue increases and customer loyalty gained by ensuring their response team is attentive and effective.

    We cater to sales teams, customer service teams, job recruiters, medical offices, human resources departments, and much more!

    What kind of reports do I get?

    Users get a monthly report of their inbox's reply times with customized reporting, PDF export options, and the ability to define business hours.

    Premium users get advanced data filtering, team and shared inbox level reporting, and support for data export in various formats.

    Teams users get a whole host of additional features not limited to advanced reporting with historical data, allowing for month-to-month trend reports and agent comparison/leaderboard reports.

    I only want to show stats for certain emails, is that doable?

    Yes, Email Reply Time always automatically discounts emails marked as spam in reply metrics.

    We support filtering for Gmail labels and specfic recipients, as well as in-depth sorting & filtering options.

    Is my information secure?

    Your information is secure because we do not have access to all of it!

    Our solution only analyzes the metadata of time metrics and the subject lines of emails.

    Email body content and attachments are not readable via our terms of use and in accordance with Google Gmail & Workspace API usage. Elements like the time response metadata and subject line data are encrypted at rest.

    Our users login directly via Google and our application is audited and approved by Google in accordance with their terms of use.

    Click here to learn more about Email Reply Time and Team Cloud Suite security.

    Will I notice a difference in my inbox functionality?

    Not at all. The Team Cloud Suite app queries your Gmail's metadata with the Google Workspace API and it cannot alter any functionality.

    Is any software installation required?

    No.

    Do I need a credit card to sign up?

    No, a credit card is not required to sign up for Email Reply Time or any Team Cloud Suite products. We encourage any interested users in seeing the power of our Email Reply Time tool for themselves.

    A payment method is only required once you decide to become a premium or teams member.
