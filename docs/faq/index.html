What does Team Cloud Suite Provide?

Team Cloud Suite is an offical Cloud Partner of Google Workspace.

As a Google Workspace Partner, we offer the tools you need to migrate to us with the addition of dedicated account support and a free suite of business productivity tools!

Google relies on companies like us to ensure their users have a smooth Workspace experience.

Whether you are new with Google Workspace, already a user, or with another manager provider, our tool makes it easy to migrate to Team Cloud Suite.

There are no addtional costs and you can opt out at any time with the click of the button.

Team Cloud Suite users get Email Reply Time, a tool that allows for instant insights into an organization's reply time habits. Customize your reporting, filter effectivley for the cleanest data possible, and quickly spot the weakpoints in your team's repsonse habits.

Increase your bottom line, streamline interaction touchpoints, and delight your customer base with Email Reply Time.

Our users also get Team Signature Change, a powerful tool that allows for quick and painless edits of email signatures. Easily add new company logos, locations, job titles, or contact information by the individual, a set group, or company-wide.

How does your migration tool work?

For users that already use Google Workspace and want to use our tools for free, they can migrate their Workspace account to us.

Our sign up process will take you step-by-step to locate your unique Workspace token under your Google Admin Account.

You will provide that token to our tool and our application will do the rest.

Your Workspace account will then be associated with Team Cloud Suite and you will get our tools for absolutely zero cost.

What happens when we make Team Cloud Suite our managed provider?

Like all Workspace managed providers, the Team Cloud Suite app will help you manage forgotten passwords, domain settings, and billing.

Nothing else changes in regards to your Workspace account.

There are no increases in costs for letting Team Cloud Suite be your Workspace manager and users can revert back to normal Workspace accounts with the click of a button.

Google Cloud partners like Team Cloud Suite allow for you to get the care and attention you need from an engaged partner, rather than solely relying on Google for support

We are simply passionate about creating business productivity tools that utilize the power of Google applications and we offer these suite of tools totally free of charge for our users.

What is the Team Cloud Suite app?

The Team Cloud Suite app is an application that runs on the Google Cloud Platform.

Through the Team Cloud Suite app, we are able to help you manage things like any forgotton passwords, adding or removing users, and more.

As a Google Cloud Platform approved app, our tool is able to work with Google to make these changes easily.

Our app also works with the Gmail API to read and analyze metadata information that we use our Email Reply Time application.

The data we read is only related to response time metrics, and through our Terms of Service agreement with Google, we cannot read things like email body information.

Email Reply Time


Frequently Asked Questions


Who is Email Reply Time for?

Email Reply Time for those who want instant insights into their email response time analytics.

Our customers see the value in the revenue increases and customer loyalty gained by ensuring their response team is attentive and effective.

We cater to sales teams, customer service teams, job recruiters, medical offices, human resources departments, and much more!

What kind of reports do I get?

Users get a monthly report of their inbox's reply times with PDF export and an option to set business hours.

Additionally, users get customized reporting, advanced data filtering, team and shared inbox level reporting, and support for data export in various formats.

Enterprise users get a whole host of additional features not limited to advanced reporting with historical data, allowing for month-to-month trend reports and agent comparison/leaderboard reports.

I only want to show stats for certain emails, is that doable?

Yes, Email Reply Time always automatically discounts emails marked as spam in reply metrics.

We support filtering for Gmail labels and specfic recipients, as well as in-depth sorting & filtering options.

Is my information secure?

Your information is secure because we do not have access to all of it! Our solution only analyzes the metadata of time metrics and the subject lines of emails.

Email body content and attachments are not readable via our terms of use and in accordance with Google Gmail & Workspace API usage. Elements like the time response metadata and subject line data are encrypted at rest.

Our users login via Google and our application is audited and approved by Google in accordance with their terms of use.

Click here to learn more about Email Reply Time security.

Will I notice a difference in my inbox functionality?

Not at all. Email Reply Time queries your Gmail's metadata with our API and it cannot and does not alter the functionality.

Is any software installation required?

No.
